About Me

I’m a 3D Artist with over three years of hands-on production experience, specializing in modeling, texturing, lighting, and realistic visualization. My work centers on building high-quality, optimized 3D assets for product visualization, AR workflows, and immersive digital environments. I focus heavily on clean topology, accurate UVs, and physically-based texturing so assets hold up in both real-time and high-resolution renders.

My toolkit includes Maya, Blender, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Quixel Mixer, AutoCAD, 3ds Max, and rendering engines like V-Ray, Arnold, and Cycles. I’m also experienced with AR-ready asset optimization, lighting setups, prop and environment design, and early-stage concept visualization.

What drives my work is simple: I like turning ideas into visuals that actually make people stop and look. I’m constantly improving my workflow, learning new tools, and pushing myself to build cleaner, faster, and more believable models. If a project demands precision, efficiency, and strong visual storytelling, that’s where I do my best work.
